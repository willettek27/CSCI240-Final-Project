Kathryn Willette

Kathryn Willette is a 22 years old and is currently studying Computer Science at Rockford University. She has played violin since she was 5 years old and has played in many orchestras both in school and on the side. Since middle school, she has been in 5 different orchestras such as the Rockford Youth Symphony Orchestra (RYSO), Music Academy's Measure 5 Orchestra, and both regular/honors orchestra in school.

She has also competed in the Mendelsson Competition in elementary and have won 2nd, 3rd, and 4th places. In elementary school, middle school and high school, she has gotten 1st place every year in Solo Ensemble. She has also competed for placement in District in middle school and high school. With the high chair placement in High School, she was able to perform in ILMEC All-State in Peoria 10th to 12th grade.
